Mary and June were sitting on the train at the start of their trip. "Tell me what?" demanded Mary.

"Its just that....." said June "Well, we'll be sharing a room and possibly the same bed and.." she hesitated again.

"Is that all?" said Mary "Well I don't mind, I often shared a bed with my sister when we went on holiday."

"Yes, that's all very well" said June "But did either of you wet it" she blurted out.

"Not since we were kids" admitted Mary. But why do you ask?"

"Cool" said Helen "It's great that we met you and that you wet yourselves as well" and she too drank from her water bottle.

"I can't hold on any longer" blurted Helen "and I don't know if I'll be able to stop once I get started."

"Yes, we met on the train here" said June. "But what's up?"

"Well we have a bit of a problem" replied Janet. "The computer went down and somehow we overbooked for most of the summer. The only solution I can see is if we all sleep in the barn and I use our rooms for the campers" she explained. "I would of course pay a bonus for that and I will give you some cash which you can spend on some air beds. Or" she added "glancing at Helen's jumper "if you like, you can keep the cash and we can use the old mattresses that are out there in the barn. They're still comfortable they just have a few stains."

"They'll have a lot more by the end of the summer" said June with a grin. "We won't have to protect them. Our wee will run straight through and soak into the floor out in the barn and we won't have to clear up under our beds in the morning."

"I've been wetting my knickers as long as I can remember" admitted Sue "and introduced Helen to it a few days ago, but we haven't wet our beds yet. I'm looking forward to that and can hardly wait until tonight."
